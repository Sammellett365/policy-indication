DETAILS ▼
All benefits are underwritten by certain Underwriters at Lloyd’s of London
Tailoring your policy. ▼
The Latitudes Crew program includes various components which can be combined as desired. At a minimum, the policy must include Medical or Disability & Accidental Death cover. The Health Plus benefit (routine/preventive check-ups, dental care and optical services and supplies) Death due to Illness and Hospital Daily Indemnity are all available as optional extras. The Disability & Accidental Death cover can be structured in various ways, including or excluding cover for loss of wages or permanent disablement due to illness.
Premium Payment ▼
Premium payments are annual, with crew changes adjusted at expiry on a pro‑rata basis.
Administration of Crew Changes ▼
Provided a crew complement of at least three is maintained, new hires should be notified to us by e-mail to include name, date of birth, nationality, position, salary and start date. If the crew complement starts out below three, individual forms would be required. Upon receipt of the notification of new crew, we provide an individual Declaration of Insurance (PDF) for the new member which includes confirmation of the cover and the numbers to call in the event of a claim. For departing crew, an e-mail confirming the name of the crew member and the date the coverage should terminate is sufficient. We will be happy to assist with individual plans where needed.
Claim Payments ▼
Claim payments can be made directly to the medical provider (subject to their agreement), to the employer or to the crew member, as desired. Claims handling, Emergency Assistance and US cost containment is provided by Global Medical Management, Inc. in Florida, a Europ Assistance company, supported by MHG's staff in the Isle of Man, Fort Lauderdale and Hamburg.
